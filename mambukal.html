MAMBUKAL RESORT

Mambukal Resort — Nature and Relaxation Combined

Mambukal Resort is a well-known mountain destination located in Murcia, Negros Occidental. Surrounded by lush forests and fresh mountain air, the resort offers a peaceful escape from the busy city.

It is one of the most visited nature spots in the province, known for its relaxing atmosphere and natural attractions.

Relax in Natural Hot Springs

One of the main highlights of Mambukal Resort is its natural hot sulfur springs. Visitors can enjoy soaking in warm pools that are believed to help relieve stress and relax the body.

The hot springs are a perfect way to unwind after exploring the resort’s outdoor attractions.

Explore Waterfalls and Trails

Mambukal is famous for its seven waterfalls, which can be reached through hiking trails. Each level offers a different view and experience, making it a great activity for adventure seekers.

The trails are surrounded by greenery, allowing visitors to enjoy nature while trekking through the area.

Outdoor Activities and Wildlife

The resort offers various outdoor activities such as boating, ziplining, and visiting the butterfly garden. These activities provide both fun and educational experiences for visitors.

You can also spot different species of birds and insects, adding to the natural charm of the place.

Why Visit Mambukal Resort?

Mambukal Resort is ideal for those who want a balance of relaxation and adventure. It combines natural beauty with enjoyable activities, making it a complete getaway destination.

Whether you want to hike, relax in hot springs, or simply enjoy nature, Mambukal offers a refreshing experience in Negros Occidental.
